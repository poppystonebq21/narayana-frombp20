# SEO Optimization Report - Narayana Physio Care

## Overview

Comprehensive SEO optimization performed to rank for physiotherapy-related searches across all Gurugram localities. The goal is to appear in top results when someone searches for a physiotherapist or physiotherapy services in Gurugram and all nearby areas.

---

## 1. Structured Data (Schema.org JSON-LD)

### MedicalBusiness Schema (index.html)
- Full business profile with name, alternate names, description
- Two clinic addresses (Sector 109 + Palam Vihar)
- Opening hours (7 days, morning + evening sessions)
- Phone numbers, payment methods, price range
- Logo and business image
- **areaServed**: 24+ specific localities in Gurugram listed individually (Sector 109, Palam Vihar, Dwarka Expressway, Sohna Road, Golf Course Road, DLF Phase 1-5, Manesar, South City, Sushant Lok, Huda City Centre, Nirvana Country, Vatika, etc.)
- 8 available medical services with descriptions
- Employee (Dr. Anju Jakhar) with qualifications and specializations
- Aggregate rating (4.9 stars, 150+ reviews)
- Offer catalog for services

### FAQPage Schema (index.html)
- 8 frequently asked questions with complete answers
- Covers: sessions needed, pain concerns, home visits, location, conditions treated, timings
- Enables FAQ rich snippets in Google search results

### BreadcrumbList Schema (index.html)
- Site navigation hierarchy for Google search breadcrumbs
- Lists all main pages: Home > Services > Conditions > About > Contact

### Physician Schema (index.html)
- Dedicated doctor profile schema
- Name, qualifications, specializations, workplace
- Helps appear in "doctors near me" searches

## 5. Technical SEO

### robots.txt (public/robots.txt)
- Allows all crawlers
- Points to sitemap location
- Sets polite crawl-delay

### sitemap.xml (public/sitemap.xml)
- Lists all 9 pages with URLs
- Priority values based on importance (homepage = 1.0, services/conditions/locations = 0.9)
- Change frequency hints for crawlers

### Canonical URLs
- Every page has a canonical URL to prevent duplicate content issues
- All pointing to the production domain

### Robots Meta Tag
- `index, follow` on all pages
- `max-snippet:-1` for unlimited snippet length
- `max-image-preview:large` for large image previews in search
- `max-video-preview:-1` for unlimited video previews
